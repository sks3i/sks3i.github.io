A audio spectogram is a visual representation of how intensity of various frequencies varies over time. Some commonly used spectograms in audio processing are:
1. Linear spectogram
2. Mel spectogram
3. Log-frequency spectogram
4. Constant-Q Transform (CQT) spectogram

## Linear Spectogram

Linear spectogram shows how power of various frequencies varies over time, with frequencies spaced evenly apart. Since the frequencies are evenly spaced, the high frequency component can be discerned clearly while it is harder to discern lower frequencies.
